About Us



Education For Liberia (EFL) is a nonprofit organization formed by students, for students. This group of college students came together with the common goal for the bettering of the educational system in Liberia, West Africa. In order to make a mark on the future of Liberia, they sought out to work for the children of today through knowledge, in hopes of making an impact on their futures tomorrow.

Having migrated to the United States in hopes of a better education, these students have seen the impact of education of their own lives. this outlook has become the motivation in making the same progress for the children of Liberia. We believe that change stems from one positive step to another and cannot be done by a single organization alone. Furthermore, we believe with the support of the community this organization could enrich the lives of many unprivileged students.
